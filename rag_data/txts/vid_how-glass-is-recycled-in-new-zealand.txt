Title: How glass is recycled in New Zealand
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=Cgoe6X_pAcU

Most Kiwis can now recycle glass bottles and jars from home. Give them a rinse of their dirty, then put them out for recycling. Glass bottles are separated out at the recycling plant. Some of our glass goes to Auckland where it is sorted by colour, crushed and melted. Then turn into new bottles. So take off the lid and rinse dirty jars and bottles before recycling. Get up to date on recycling rights.
