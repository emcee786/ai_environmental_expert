Almost all Kiwis can now recycle food tins and drink cans from home. Dirty tins and cans just need a quick rinse before recycling. Tins and cans are sorted at the recycling plant. Before they are crushed, shredded, then melted, and finally turned into new cans and other metal products. So rinse out your dirty tins and cans and put them out for recycling.
